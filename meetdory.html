Meet Dory, your cafe companion.

Dory is a 15 year old domestic shorthair cat. She loves to nap, cuddle, and play.

A space designed to entertain.

The Lounge is Dory's favorite place to hang out. Snuggle on the sofa with ample throw pillows and blankets, or batt around Dory's toy mice on the plush rug.

The Dining area features a full bar and barstools as well as a dining table. Elegant geometric decor will inspire you as you drink and dine.
